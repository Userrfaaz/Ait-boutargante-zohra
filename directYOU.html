Primary locations

Germany

This is Porsche's home country, where the automaker was founded in 1931. Car production began in 1948 with the Porsche 356.

Slovakia

Porsche has an assembly plant in Bratislava, where the Porsche Cayenne has been manufactured since 2012. 1

Finland

The Finnish car manufacturer Valmet Automotive has been involved in the production of Porsche cars since the 1990s.

Austria

Magna Steyr, a large Austrian supplier, has collaborated with Porsche since the early 2000s.

Chine

Porsche has a growing presence in China, but it's not primarily for manufacturing. China is a key market for Porsche

Etats-Unis

Porsche also has research and development centers in the U.S., like the Porsche Experience Center in Atlanta and Los Angeles
