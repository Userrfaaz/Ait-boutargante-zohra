The Panamera Turbo E-Hybrid's 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine

The combustion engine of the Panamera Turbo E-Hybrid is a new 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine that delivers a maximum power of 382 kW (519 hp).
